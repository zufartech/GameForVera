Train your brain by Zufar and Raduan
Your target is to reveal by logic prediction the five-digit hidden number  that the app generated.
There are two hints Z and R, where Z= exact position of a digit in a row
                                                                     R=wrong position of a digit in a row

e.g.  app generated  12345
                your trial      97205 – 1R 1Z 
                                        42865 – 2Z
                                        32145 – 2R3Z
                                        12345 – 5Z&#13;&#10
